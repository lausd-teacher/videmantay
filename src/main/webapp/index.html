Elementary School App


Welcome Stakeholders!

This App is designed to open lines of communication between the educators, care takers and students of our great school.


Before you can begin, you will need to have a school email address - For teachers: Done! You got one when you were hired.
For care takers: Every student in the district is automatically assigned an account, if you need to activate your child's account please go to Student Email page and enter the correct data. If you need help please ask the office or see Mr. ViDemantay.


Now for some bad news... The App is in beta and only invited users will be able to access it. Please be patient during this initial phase which may take up to a year. Thank you in advance for your understanding.



Classroom and Learning Management System Built on Google Apps

That's right! This App is built on top of Google Apps which means you use your school SSO to login. It also means better security and protection because Google has it on lock.
